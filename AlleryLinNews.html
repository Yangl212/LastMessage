Teen Found Dead in Riverside Home, Investigation Ongoing

Authorities say there is no immediate evidence of foul play, though the death of Riverside High School student Allery Lin remains under review.

New York, NY April 15 Staff Report

New York, NY — April 15

A 16-year-old high school student, identified as Allery Lin, was found dead in her home late Sunday evening, according to local authorities. Emergency services responded to a call from a family member and pronounced her deceased at the scene.

Preliminary reports suggest the cause of death was self-inflicted injuries. Officials stated that there is currently no evidence of foul play, though the case remains under review.

Lin was a student at Riverside High School and was described by peers as quiet and academically driven. School representatives declined to comment in detail but confirmed that counseling services have been made available to students.

Neighbors reported limited recent interaction with the family, noting that Lin had not been seen outside frequently in recent weeks.

Authorities have not released further details at this time. The investigation is ongoing.

Case Status

Ongoing review. Authorities say no confirmed evidence of foul play has been identified so far.

School Response

Riverside High School confirmed counseling resources are being made available to students following the incident.

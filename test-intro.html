How to Play

Everything within reach
can be touched.

Talk to members. Mine conversations for clues.
Map out relationships through what's said — and what isn't.
There is more than one way out.

目之所及，均可交互。
你可以选择与成员对话，从聊天记录中获取线索或通过推演厘清人物关系。
通关的方法不止一种。

Content Warning

This game contains flashing imagery,
disturbing themes, and psychological horror.

Players with epilepsy or sensitivity to flashing lights are advised not to play. Themes include missing persons, online manipulation, and ambiguous morality.

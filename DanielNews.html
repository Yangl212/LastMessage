A student death that raises more questions than answers.

Investigators say prolonged bullying, emotional neglect, and misleading online influence may all have contributed to the death of a 15-year-old student from Westbrook High School.

Neighbors described the household as quiet. Several residents said they rarely saw the boy outside except when leaving for school.

News Report

A 15-year-old boy, a student at Westbrook High School, was found dead at home on May 12, 2024, in what authorities have preliminarily ruled as a suicide by wrist laceration.

According to the initial investigation, the victim had been subjected to prolonged bullying, which is believed to have had a significant negative impact on his mental health and may have contributed to the incident.

The boy was raised in a single-parent household by his father. Investigations indicate that due to long working hours, the father paid limited attention to the boy’s psychological condition and failed to recognize warning signs in time.

Meanwhile, the boy had gradually been exposed to misleading content online and, without proper guidance, became increasingly involved, ultimately leading to the tragedy.

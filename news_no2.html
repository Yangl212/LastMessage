15-Year-Old Girl Dies After Accidental Fall; Investigation Ongoing

A 15-year-old girl died on Tuesday evening after an accidental fall from a residential building, local authorities confirmed.

Emergency services responded to the scene shortly after 5:00 p.m. Paramedics transported the teenager to a nearby hospital, where she was later pronounced dead due to severe injuries.

Police have stated that the incident is currently under investigation. Preliminary findings indicate no immediate signs of foul play, though officials emphasized that inquiries are ongoing and additional details will be released once confirmed.

The school the student attended issued a brief statement expressing condolences to the family and noting that counseling resources would be made available to students and staff affected by the incident.

Authorities reminded the public to prioritize safety and to seek professional support in times of crisis. Anyone with relevant information is encouraged to contact local law enforcement.

Further updates will be provided as the investigation continues.
